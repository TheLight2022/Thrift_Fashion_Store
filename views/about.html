Our History

Our story begins with a problem (the best ideas usually do). About ten years ago, our founder, then she was a student, saving money on clothes was essential. So she started to have ideas about shopping, reusing used fashion items.

In 2015, our founder came to Vietnam and she was very surprised by the second-hand shops here. They have high to very high sales volume, almost always crowded with customers, especially on weekends. Then, when she came back, she started a chain of stores selling used fashion according to the model seen in Vietnam.

In 2022, after 3 years of the Covid-19 epidemic, it became more difficult to buy used fashion because stores were forced to close their business to ensure the prevention of the epidemic. Thus, Thrift Fashion Store - an online shopping store for used fashion items was born

Our Approach

We’re taking positive steps to reduce our impact on the planet. For us, that means retailing responsibly. We’ve set a number of goals to get us there.

Our commitment to great design has extended into making better business choices that minimise our impact on people and the planet. For 2021, we’re focusing on four key areas where we can really make a difference. This is just the start of our journey, so keep checking back for updates.

Things like: eliminating all unnecessary plastic from our product packaging, and ensuring that the most environmentally impactful materials we use are 100% sustainably sourced.
